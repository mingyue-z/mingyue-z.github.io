About Me

I am an Associate Professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, specializing in management science, data analysis, and artificial intelligence applications. My research focuses on optimizing productivity, recommendation systems, and analyzing user behavior in online platforms.

Research Areas

  • Recommendation Systems and Bias Mitigation
  • Large Language Models and Human-AI Collaboration
  • Digital Platform Optimization and Information Ecology
  • Behavioral Complexity and Decision Making

Teaching

I have been teaching at the university level for over three years, with excellent student evaluations. Courses I teach include:

  • Computational Text Analysis
  • Data Science and Business Analytics
  • Management Theories and Methods

Selected Publications

  • "Unsupervised Anomaly Detection on Attributed Multiplex Heterogeneous Networks" - Published in Journal of AI Research.
  • "Beyond The Financial Value: How Crowdfunding Predicts Market Performance" - A study using Kickstarter data.
  • "Sunk Cost Fallacy, Price Adjustment, and Subscription Service" - Examining behavioral economics in subscription services.
