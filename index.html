About Me

I am an Associate Professor in School of Business and Management at Shanghai International Studies University. My research interests focus on human-AI collaboration, online communities, incentive mechinism in UGC platforms, and product recommendations. Recent publications include papers in MIS Quarterly, Decision Sciences, ACM Transactions on Knowledge Discovery from Data (TKDD), Decision Support Systems, Information & Management etc.
